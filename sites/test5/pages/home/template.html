Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about getting started with Guardz.

How does Guardz differ from traditional antivirus?

Unlike traditional antivirus that relies on signature-based detection, Guardz uses AI and behavioral analysis to detect sophisticated threats in real-time. Our agentic platform doesn't just detect — it actively responds to threats across email, endpoints, and cloud environments.

Do I need to install agents on client devices?

Guardz offers zero-agent deployment for many security controls, particularly for email and cloud security. For endpoint protection, we offer lightweight agents that install silently without disrupting end-users. Most MSPs are fully deployed within 30 minutes.

Can I white-label the Guardz platform?

Absolutely. Guardz is designed for MSPs with full white-label capabilities. You can brand the client portal, security reports, and email notifications with your own logo and colors. Your clients see your brand, not ours.

What happens when a threat is detected?

Our 24/7 security operations team investigates every alert, automatically remediates confirmed threats, and keeps you informed through real-time notifications and detailed incident reports. You stay in control while we handle the heavy lifting.

How does the pricing work for MSPs?

We offer flexible, per-user pricing that scales with your business. You can package Guardz services into your existing offerings and create new recurring revenue streams. Contact our team for custom MSP pricing and partnership options.
